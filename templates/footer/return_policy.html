Return Policy

Due to the delicate nature of our handcrafted products, especially gypsum items, we follow a strict return policy.

  • Products can only be returned if they arrive damaged or defective.
  • Customers must provide unboxing video proof to claim damage.
  • Claims must be made within 24 hours of delivery.
  • We are not responsible for damages caused by poor handling by third-party delivery services.
  • No return or exchange is applicable for change of mind.
  • We strongly recommend checking the package carefully upon delivery.
